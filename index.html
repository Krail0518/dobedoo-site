The Story

A drink
first.
everything else second.

Most infused drinks taste like the thing they are — either chalky and medicinal, or a sugar-bomb in a cartoon can. We wanted to make one we'd actually want to drink.

So we started with the lemonade. Real lemons, cane sugar, a short ingredient list, the recipe pulled apart and rebuilt until it tasted like the one your grandmother made on the porch. Then we added precision nano-THC — 5mg, even across every can, fast to come on and easy to put down.

The drink comes first. The lift is the bonus.
